Chocolate Cake

A simple and delicious chocolate cake recipe for beginners.
Perfect for birthdays and celebrations!
Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180oC.
  2. Slowlymix the flour, sugar, and cocoa powder.
  3. Add eggs and milk, stir until smooth.
  4. Bake for 30 minutesorr until a toothpick comes out clean

Tips

For extra flavor, add chocolate chipsbefore baking.
You can also serve with vanilla ice cream for a delicious combo!
